Critical analysis helps individuals and organisations avoid falling victim to misleading or false information. By scrutinizing data and argument inconsistencies, biases, and manipulations that might distort the truth can be found.

One such book which illuminates this topic is 'How to lie with statistics' by Darrell Huff written in 1954. The book aims to educate readers about the common ways in which statistics can be distorted or presented in a misleading manner. Another great paper which discusses the dangers of not critically evaulating article is 'Storks Deliver Babies' by Robert Matthews of which a PDF copy is linked here.
